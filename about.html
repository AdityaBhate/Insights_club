About us
Welcome to Insights Club, where journalism meets the community at VIT Bhopal University. Insights’ is dedicated to curating and sharing the stories that shape our world, the events that captivate us, and the ideas that ignite curiosity. Its distinct offering is a monthly newsletter that captures the pulse of ongoing, upcoming, and completed happenings, from enlightening seminars to vibrant fests and more.
The mission of Insights Club is to ensure a seamless flow of information that directly reaches the university's vibrant student community and spreads throughout. From the latest campus events to pressing current affairs, Insights keeps the audience informed through modern online channels like websites and social media and through the immersive world of on-field journalism and monthly newsletters. This website will increase the horizon of Insights Club beyond mail.
But Insights is not just about reporting but involving the community. Students are encouraged to join a diverse range of intra-campus events that welcome participants from every corner of the academic landscape. Together, they cultivate skills that fuel the creation of innovative and impactful media messages.
So, let us embark on this journey of discovery and storytelling. We bring you all the monthly newsletters and our magazine to you at one stop. Together, the community uncovers the essence of university life and learning. Join Insights, your gateway to a world of knowledge and connection.
